Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

DayPage was built with your privacy at its core. We believe your personal journaling is deeply private, and we designed our app to respect that. DayPage is a local-first app -- your journal entries, moods, and reflections stay on your device by default.

DayPage stores all data locally on your device by default. Optional iCloud Sync and Duo Mode features are fully opt-in and under your control.

Data Stored Locally

DayPage stores the following information on your device using Apple's secure storage:

  • Journal Entries: Morning check-ins, evening reflections, and weekly reviews
  • Quick Notes: Short notes you capture throughout the day
  • Mood Data: Your mood tracking selections and history
  • Streaks: Your journaling streak counts and dates
  • Custom Prompts: Personalized prompts you create
  • App Settings: Your preferences for themes, dark mode, notifications, and widgets

iCloud Sync (Optional)

DayPage offers optional iCloud synchronization to keep your journal entries synced across your Apple devices. When enabled:

  • Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Syncing uses Apple's end-to-end encrypted iCloud infrastructure
  • We never have access to your iCloud data
  • You can enable or disable cloud sync at any time in Settings

iCloud Sync is entirely optional. You can use DayPage fully offline with all data stored only on your device.

Duo Mode (Optional)

DayPage includes an optional Duo Mode that lets you journal together with a partner. When you opt into Duo Mode:

  • You choose which entries to share with your connected partner
  • Shared entries are transmitted securely between paired accounts
  • You can disconnect from Duo Mode at any time
  • Remote notifications are used to alert your partner of shared updates

Duo Mode is entirely opt-in. You must explicitly connect with a partner and choose what to share.

Notifications

DayPage uses notifications for:

  • Morning and evening journaling reminders (local, optional)
  • Streak milestone celebrations (local)
  • Duo Mode partner updates (remote, only when Duo Mode is active)

You can customize or disable all notifications in Settings or in your device's notification preferences.

Information We Do NOT Collect

DayPage does not collect, access, or transmit:

  • Your location data
  • Your contacts or address book
  • Your browsing history
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Any data to third-party analytics or advertising services

In-App Purchases

DayPage offers optional premium features via in-app purchases. These transactions are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

Your Data, Your Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Cloud Sync Toggle: Enable or disable iCloud sync at any time
  • Duo Mode: Choose exactly what you share with your partner
  • PDF Export: Export your entries as PDF files locally on your device
  • Notifications: Customize which notifications you receive
  • Delete: Remove individual entries or all data at any time
  • Full Removal: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

Children's Privacy

DayPage is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since data is stored locally on the device by default and is not transmitted to us, we have no way to identify or collect information from any users, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

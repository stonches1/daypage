Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

FreshPath is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We believe your personal journey to break habits is deeply private, and we've built our app to respect that.

FreshPath does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. All your data stays on your device.

Information We Store Locally

FreshPath stores the following information only on your device using Apple's secure on-device storage:

  • Habit Data: Names of habits you're tracking, streak counts, check-in dates, and milestones
  • Journal Entries: Your personal journal notes and mood selections
  • Progress Photos: Images you choose to save for tracking your journey
  • Future Letters: Messages you write to your future self
  • Profile Information: Your chosen display name and avatar (customizable, not required)
  • App Settings: Your preferences for notifications, themes, and reminders

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it using the app's export feature.

Information We Do NOT Collect

FreshPath does not collect, access, or transmit:

  • Your location data
  • Your contacts or address book
  • Your browsing history
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Any health data from HealthKit
  • Any data to third-party analytics services

Notifications

FreshPath may send you local notifications for:

  • Daily motivation reminders (optional)
  • Evening check-in reminders (optional)
  • Custom habit reminders you set
  • Milestone celebrations

These notifications are generated locally on your device. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in the app settings or your device's Settings app.

In-App Purchases

FreshPath offers optional premium features through in-app purchases. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

Data Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: Use the Export feature in Settings to save your data
  • Delete: Delete individual habits or entries at any time
  • Complete Removal: Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device

Children's Privacy

FreshPath is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally on the device and not transmitted to us, we have no way to identify or collect information from any users, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
